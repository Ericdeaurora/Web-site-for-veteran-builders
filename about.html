About Us

Veteran Builders is a veteran-owned company founded by Eric DeAurora and Philip Marchman. With a strong background in both construction and military service, we are committed to delivering quality craftsmanship, integrity, and customer satisfaction in every project we undertake.

We specialize in custom decks, outdoor living spaces, and residential remodeling. Based in the heart of Georgia, we proudly serve the Lake Oconee and Lake Sinclair areas. Every project we complete reflects our dedication to transforming ideas into reality while building trust and long-lasting relationships with our clients.
