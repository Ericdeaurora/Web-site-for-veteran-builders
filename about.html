About Veteran Builders

Veteran Builders is proudly owned and operated by Eric DeAurora and Phillip Marchman. As veterans, we bring our discipline, dedication, and integrity to every project we undertake. Specializing in custom decks, outdoor living spaces, and home remodeling, we combine decades of experience with a dedication to turning your vision into reality.

Serving the Lake Oconee and Lake Sinclair areas, we understand the unique charm and needs of lakeside living. Our projects not only enhance the functionality and beauty of your property but also add long-term value, seamlessly blending with the surrounding environment.

We are committed to adhering to the highest industry standards, ensuring every project meets rigorous quality checks and surpasses client expectations. Transparency is at the core of our process—clients are kept informed every step of the way, from initial consultation to the final walkthrough. At Veteran Builders, we build more than structures; we build lasting relationships through trust, excellence, and superior service.
